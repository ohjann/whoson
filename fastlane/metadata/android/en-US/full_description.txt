WhosOn is an open-source festival schedule planner that works completely offline.

Plan your festival experience by importing schedules from Clashfinder or uploading your own JSON/CSV files. Highlight acts you want to see, detect clashes between overlapping sets, and get notified before your favourite artists take the stage.

Features:
- Import festival schedules from Clashfinder or custom JSON/CSV
- Highlight acts with priority levels and personal notes
- Visual clash detection for overlapping sets
- Now Playing and Up Next view for the current stage
- Local notifications so you never miss a set
- Multiple festival support
- Fully offline — all data stored locally on your device
- No accounts, no tracking, no ads
- Open source (MIT License)

WhosOn respects your privacy. All data stays on your device using IndexedDB storage. No analytics, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs.
